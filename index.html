Projects

Some Projects That I Am Currently Working On:
Capstone - Individual Project - DoNest

DoNEST will be an application that allows people to donate to low-income families in their community through the loo app. Donators will be able to submit photos of the items that they want to donate, once the items are accepted in the app users will be able to drop donations off at the local hub. Through the app donators will be able to see an itemized list of their donation(s). People or organizations in need of donations will be able to submit posts, stories and or causes (with goal amounts / items or deadlines) that will be open for donations. They will also be able to reserve items from the available inventory at their local hub.

Capstone - Group Project - IS&T Capstone Project

We are working with an internal Temple client Rob Zahorchak to create a system that will allow the professor / Temple University to find external clients (non-profits) that could be brought into the pipeline for systems / projects to be created for the Capstone courses.
